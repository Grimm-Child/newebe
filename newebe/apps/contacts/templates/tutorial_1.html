Tutorial

You have actually no contact. A contact is represented by its URL. If you met someone and want to add it as a newebe contact, just ask him its newebe URL. Type it in the field on top of the page, then click on the add button or type enter.
Once this is done your contact appears with a pending status. It means you must wait that your contact confirms you to start communicating with him. Sometimes you will see contact request. It means some wants to add you as its contact. You can accept this by clicking on the confirm button displayed on the contact line when you put your mouse over the line of this contact.
If you don't want to share something with this contact you can simply delete it.
