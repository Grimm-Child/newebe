Tutorial

You have no micro posts yet. To start you should add contacts by going to the contact page. Just click on the word contact in the top navigation menu to do that.

If you already have some, it's time to micro post. What is posting ? It is just the fact to write little stories for your contacts.

To makes micro posts, type some text message in the text area on top of this page then click on the post button or type ctrl + enter.
